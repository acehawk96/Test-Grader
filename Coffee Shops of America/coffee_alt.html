Not a coffee drinker or maybe taking a break from coffee? There are many other alternative beverages to coffee. We recommend trying bubble tea, smoothies,and matcha tea.

  1. Bubble tea is a delishious tea based drink that is combined with milk or fruit flavoring and poured over chewy tapioca pearls known as boba.
  2. Smoothies are a commonly known beverage and can be be made with fruit and icecream or pretty much any combination you could think of.
  3. Matcha tea is powdered green tea leaves brewed with hot water for a little caffine kick for your morning or mid-day routine.
